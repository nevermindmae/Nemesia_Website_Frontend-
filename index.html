What is Nemesia?

The name Nemesia is a deliberate nod to Nemesis, the Greek goddess associated with balance, rightful consequence and the restoration of equilibrium when things tip too far.

In aesthetic medicine, personal decisions can be negatively influenced by hype, bias and misinformation, leading to undesired outcomes and further driving feelings of dysmorphia.

Nemesia was founded to restore the equilibrium and help promote a realistic and scientifically-sound outlook on aesthetic medicine.

At Nemesia, we believe that just like the delicate blooms of nemesia, a beautiful flower by the same name, beauty thrives best under optimal and balanced conditions.
